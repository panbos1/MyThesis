Interactive Weaving Workshop

Date: 10 December 2025

Time: 17:00 – 20:00

Location: Folk Art Workshop, Old Town of Rethymno

This workshop is aimed at anyone who wants to experience the art of weaving in practice. In the specially designed space of the Folk Art Workshop, there are both small and large looms, giving participants the chance to work with real equipment.

During the workshop, participants will learn the basic steps of setting up a loom, traditional weaving techniques, yarn selection and color combinations. By the end, everyone will create a small woven piece that they can take home.

Participation fee: €8 (all materials included)

Places: 20 — Booking required
