Evening of Tradition & Weaving Heritage

Date: 20 January 2026

Time: 19:00 – 21:30

Location: Anogeia Folklore Museum, Heritage Hall

A special cultural evening dedicated to the precious weaving heritage of Anogeia. In a space filled with history and authenticity, visitors will experience live weaving demonstrations, discover the area’s characteristic motifs and hear stories passed down from generation to generation.

The event includes a presentation on the history of weaving, an exhibition of authentic textiles and a discussion on preserving and reviving tradition within local communities. At the end, traditional Cretan treats will be offered.

Admission: Free
